kops

kops stands for Kubernetes Operations. It allows you to do production grade Kubernetes installations, upgrades and management. kops only works on Mac/Linux.

kops will not only help you create, destroy, upgrade and maintain production-grade, highly available, Kubernetes cluster, but it will also provision the necessary cloud infrastructure.

AWS (Amazon Web Services) is currently officially supported, with DigitalOcean, GCE, and OpenStack in beta support, and Azure and AliCloud in alpha.

Website: https://github.com/kubernetes/kops
