Example 2: 
friends = [("Rolf", 25), ("Anne", 37), ("Charlie", 31), ("Bob", 22)]
for name, age in friends:
  print(f"{name} is {age} years old")
Output: 
Rolf is 25 years old
Anne is 37 years old
Charlie is 31 years old
Bob is 22 years old
