vi clones

Nowadays, there are advanced versions of the vi editor available, and the most popular one is VIM which is Vi Improved. Some of the other ones are Elvis, Nvi, STvi (STevie), xvi. and Vile.

  • Elvis is available for: Amiga, DOS, OS/2, Unix, VMS.
  • Nvi is the vi that will ship with BSD 4.4.
  • STvi (STevie) is available for: Atari ST, DOS, Unix.
  • vim is available for: Amiga, DOS, Mac System 7, Unix. Amiga, DOS
  • xvi is available for: DOS, Unix.
  • Vile is available for: DOS, OS/2, Unix, VMS.
