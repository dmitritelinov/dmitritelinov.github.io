Test Docker image for vulnerabilities with Snyk

Download the binary: 

$ cd
$ wget https://github.com/snyk/snyk/releases/download/v1.437.3/snyk-linux
$ chmod +x ./snyk-linux
Authenticate (you'll need a valid account on snyk.io - 200 free monthly scans): 
$ ./snyk-linux auth
It will open your browser to authenticate.
Scan: 
$ ./snyk-linux container test simpleweb
