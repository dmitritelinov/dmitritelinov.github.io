MicroK8s

MicroK8s is the smallest, fastest, fully-conformant Kubernetes that tracks upstream releases and makes clustering trivial.
MicroK8s is great for offline development, prototyping, and testing. Use it on a VM as a small, cheap, reliable k8s for CI/CD. It’s also the best production grade Kubernetes for appliances. Develop IoT apps for Kubernetes and deploy them to MicroK8s on your Linux boxes. Contains Istio, Jaeger, Prometheus, Grafana, ELK etc charts out of the box.
