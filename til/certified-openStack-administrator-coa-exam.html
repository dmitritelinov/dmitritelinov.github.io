Certified OpenStack Administrator (COA) exam

The Certified OpenStack Administrator exam is the only professional certification offered by the Open Infrastructure Foundation. It was written for OpenStack professionals with at least six months of experience managing an OpenStack cloud environment.

Prerequisites:

  • IT professionals who want to demonstrate their hands-on knowledge of OpenStack
  • OpenStack experts who want to demonstrate their OpenStack skills
  • Linux System Administrators or equivalent skills are recommended
